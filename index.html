About Us

Welcome Gamers UGS is a platform for students to share and develop their passion for video games and it's associated culture. We provide a friendly and welcoming environment so that students can come and share their gaming experiences. Join us and make friends while improving your gameplay. We also hold competitive events across a wide variety of games to expand our members' experiences and knowledge, furthering the development of video game culture beyond our platform
